About Arkadia Construction

Building excellence in South Sudan since 2008 with quality, integrity, and innovation.

Building Excellence Since 2008

Arkadia Construction Company Ltd stands as a beacon of quality and reliability in South Sudan's construction industry. For over 15 years, we have been transforming landscapes and building communities with unparalleled craftsmanship and dedication.

Based in Juba, we specialize in delivering premium residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects that meet the highest international standards while respecting local architectural traditions and environmental considerations.

Our commitment extends beyond construction – we build lasting relationships with our clients, partners, and the communities we serve. With a team of highly skilled professionals and a local workforce trained to excellence, we ensure every project reflects our core values of quality, integrity, and innovation.
